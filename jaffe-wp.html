The Pelosi Minimum Wage Plan
Nancy Pelosi wants you to believe she's increasing the minimum wage from $7.25 an hour to $15 dollars an hour, but actually...

Pelosi's plan only gets you to $ in 2017 dollars. Incremental minimum wage cheats the worker because it ignores the effects of inflation. Inflation varies over the years, and it's hard to predict when it will go up or down. So if you want to make 15 dollars an hour, the minimum wage should go up to 17 dollars by 2024. Pelosi's plan just doesn't hold up to scrutiny. Jaffe has a real plan for the minimum wage that does not cheat working San Franciscans.
